Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How do I start a new game?

A: To start a new game, open the app and tap on "New Game". Enter player names and select the domino set to begin.

Q: How do I save player groups?

A: You can save frequently used player groups by going to the settings menu and selecting "Save Player Group".

Q: What domino sets are supported?

A: The app supports Double-12 and Double-15 domino sets for flexible gameplay.

Q: How do I update the app?

A: Visit the App Store and check for updates under the "Updates" section to ensure you have the latest version.

Q: Is my data secure?

A: Yes, all data is stored locally on your device and is not shared or transmitted over the internet.
